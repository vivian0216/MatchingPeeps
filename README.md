# MatchingPeeps
A simple python script to match people with eachother based on two questions: one about interests and vibes, the other about social style.

## Questions:
(Generated with ChatGPT5.1)

✅ Question 1 (Interests & Vibes)

What kind of topics do you genuinely enjoy talking about when meeting new people?

1. Music, concerts, and festivals

2. Movies/series, books, and storytelling

3. Travel, cultures, and new experiences

4. Tech, games, and nerdy hobbies

5. Sports, fitness, and outdoor activities

✅ Question 2 (Social Style)

What’s your ideal party vibe?

1. Deep 1-on-1 conversations

2. Small group chats with new people

3. Dancing and high-energy moments

4. Chilling on the couch with whoever walks by

5. Competitive fun (drinking games, card/board games, challenges)
